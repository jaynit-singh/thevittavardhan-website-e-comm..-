Billing Cycles

At Vittavardhan, we offer flexible billing cycles to accommodate the diverse needs of our clients. Our billing options include:

  • Monthly Billing:

    Ideal for clients who prefer to manage their expenses on a monthly basis. Invoices are generated on the first day of each month, with payment due within 15 days of the invoice date.

  • Quarterly Billing:

    Suitable for clients looking for a balanced approach to payment frequency. Invoices are generated on the first day of each quarter (January, April, July, October), with payment due within 15 days of the invoice date.

  • Annual Billing:

    Best for clients seeking to streamline their payments and enjoy potential discounts. Invoices are generated on the first day of the year, with payment due within 30 days of the invoice date. Clients opting for annual billing may qualify for a discount on their total annual fee.

We understand that each client has unique needs, and we are committed to providing billing flexibility to ensure a seamless experience.

Refund Policy

At Vittavardhan, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We strive to deliver high-quality software solutions that meet your expectations. However, we understand that situations may arise where a refund is necessary. Our refund policy is as follows:
