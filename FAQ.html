How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, follow these steps:

  • Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password".
  • Enter your registered email address and submit.
  • Check your email for a password reset link and follow the instructions.

What should I do if the app crashes?

If the app crashes, try the following troubleshooting steps:

  • Restart the app and see if the issue persists.
  • Check for any available updates and install them.
  • Contact our support team with details of the issue.
