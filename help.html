How do I report a technical issue?

If you encounter a technical issue, please follow these steps to report it:

  • Step 1: Gather detailed information about the issue, including error messages, screenshots, and steps to reproduce the problem.
  • Step 2: Contact our support team via email at thevittavardhan@gmail.com or call us at +91 6397598283.
  • Step 3: Provide the gathered information to our support team. They will review your report and assist you in resolving the issue promptly.

What are the system requirements for your software?

The system requirements for our software vary depending on the specific product. Generally, our software is compatible with the following systems:

  • Operating Systems: Windows 8 or later, macOS 10.01 or later, Linux (various distributions)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3 or equivalent
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM minimum (16 GB recommended)
  • Storage: 500 MB of available disk space (additional space may be required for data storage)
  • Graphics: Integrated or dedicated GPU with at least 2 GB of VRAM
  • Internet: Broadband internet connection for updates and online features

How do I update my software?

Keeping your software up to date ensures you have the latest features and security improvements. Follow these steps to update your software:

  • Step 1: Open the software and navigate to the settings or help section.
  • Step 2: Look for an option labeled "Check for Updates" or similar.
  • Step 3: If an update is available, follow the prompts to download and install it. You may need to restart the software to complete the update process.
  • Step 4: If you encounter any issues during the update process, please contact our support team for assistance.
